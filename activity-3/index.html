Melitta Single Cup Coffee Pods

Melitta coffee pods produce aromatic coffee with a smooth, rich flavor and are biodegradable. Each pod is a pre-measured serving with 7 grams of premium coffee. The pods are nitrogen flushed for maximum freshness.
