List of Fantasy Book Series

The following are lists of some of my favorite fantasy book series. The longest books in the series in terms of page numbers are shown in bold. Books that have yet to be released are shown in italics.

Wheel of Time

  1. The Eye of the World
  2. The Great Hunt
  3. The Dragon Reborn
  4. The Shadow Rising
  5. The Fires of Heaven
  6. Lord of Chaos
  7. A Crown of Swords
  8. The Path of Daggers
  9. Winter's Heart
  10. Crossroads of Twilight
  11. Prequel - New Spring1
  12. Knife of Dreams
  13. The Gathering Storm
  14. Towers of Midnight
  15. A Memory of Light

The Kingkiller Chronicle

  1. The Name of the Wind
  2. The Wise Man's Fear
  3. Novella - The Slow Regard of Silent Things2
  4. The Doors of Stone

The Stormlight Archive

  1. The Way of Kings
  2. Words of Radiance
  3. Oathbringer
  4. Stormlight Archive - Book 4


1. New Spring is a prequel of the Wheel of Time series and takes place before the first novel The Eye of the World. New Spring was released in 2004 between the release of Crossroads of Twilight and Knife of Dreams.

2. The Slow Regard of Silent Things is a novella of The Kingkiller Chronicles series and was released in 2014 following The Wise Man's Fear. This novella does not follow the original story arc, and is a seperate story highlighting one of the unique characters of the series.
